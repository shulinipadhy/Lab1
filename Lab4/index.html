About us

Thank you for visiting our website! We are Sunshine Vet Clinic, a small clinic located in Barrhaven, Ottawa.

At Sunshine Vet Clinic, we have a very passionate staff. All of our services are handled with care and are administered by a team of experienced veterinarians who love what they do. Please contact us (information at the bottom of the page) if you would like to reach out to us, or book an appointment!
